2. What is 20% of 200?

  • A. 20
  • B. 40
  • C. 60
  • D. 80

Explanation: 20% of 200 = (20/100) × 200 = 40. To calculate percentage, multiply the number by the percentage divided by 100.

3. If a train travels 300 km in 5 hours, what is its average speed?

  • A. 50 km/h
  • B. 60 km/h
  • C. 70 km/h
  • D. 80 km/h

Explanation: Average speed = Total distance ÷ Total time = 300 km ÷ 5 hours = 60 km/h.

4. A rectangle has length 8 cm and width 5 cm. What is its area?

  • A. 13 cm²
  • B. 26 cm²
  • C. 40 cm²
  • D. 45 cm²

Explanation: Area of rectangle = Length × Width = 8 cm × 5 cm = 40 cm².

5. What is the square root of 144?

  • A. 10
  • B. 11
  • C. 12
  • D. 13

Explanation: √144 = 12 because 12 × 12 = 144.
